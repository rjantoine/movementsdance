Sunday, July 17th, 2011
Edmonton's All White Affair

Highlights:
• Haute Couture Fashion Show
• Tour of the Boland Art Gallery
• Best Dressed Contest
• Scrumptious Caribbean Cuisine
• Celebrities and VIPs



This semi formal fundraiser will be held at Edmonton's renowned and award-winning flower Gardens.


This is a unique Jazz themed evening of dance and musical performances including the Marc Beaudin Jazz Trio. A special feature at our 2011 Soiree is the attendees Best Dressed Contest: Wear White and Dress to Impress! Our Celebrity judges in the know of style and the fashion industry include the Founder of Western Canada Fashion Week, Sandra Sing Fernandes.

Don't miss our City's most talented designers which include last season's Western Canada Fashion Week Emerging Designer winner, Andrea De Ocampo and the December 2010 Avenue Magazine Cover, Natasha Lazarovic. Edmonton's top designers Pauline Anderson, Lisa-Marie Couture, Jessica Halabi, Kelly Madden and Kelsey McIntyre will also showcase their design talent and artistry.

Please join our influential community leaders and Government representatives such as Mayor Stephen Mandel and his wife Lynn , and the Honourable Health Minister, Gene Zwozdesky and his wife Christine at this worthwhile Fundraiser.


Last year's event was a huge success. This year, the All White Affair is widely considered as one of the must-attend events of the summer.
