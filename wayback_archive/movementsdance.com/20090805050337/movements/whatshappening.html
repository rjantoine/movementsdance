MAS IN EDMONTON!


Looking for something fun and exciting to do this Summer?

Cariwest brings the spirit of Caribbean Carnival to the Festival City of Edmonton. Indigenous to the islands of Trinidad & Tobago, this festival is an interactive event showcasing the Caribbean’s vibrant culture, colourful costumes, and the powerful rhythms of the Steelband and Calypso music.

Tired of being a spectator??

Ever wondered how you too can join in the fun???

This year, we invite you and your friends of all ages to participate in the parade.

Wear a costume and "play mas", dance down Jasper Avenue with us. This is a fun way to exercise body parts you didn’t know you had.

You can register here, or for more information or how to purchase a costume, call our Parade Coordinator, Lisa at 780-415-5211 or email  lisa.movementsdance@shaw.ca.
