DANCE INSTRUCTOR WANTED
Movements: The Afro-Caribbean Dance Ensemble in Edmonton, Alberta, is currently accepting applications for the position of Dance Instructor for its exciting 2011 performance season.  Interested applicants should possess an extensive background in both West African and Caribbean dance disciplines with a minimum of 5 years experience.
