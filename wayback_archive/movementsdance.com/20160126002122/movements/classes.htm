Faculty:

Movements is pleased to welcome our teaching staff for the 2010 Dance Season:

QUINT JACK

Quint is a renowned choreographer, instructor and talented dancer. He recently performed as one of the lead dancers in Carnival Messiah of London, England. Quint started his dance career 21 years ago and acquired his early foundation in Modern Contemporary (Horton) at the Repertory Dance Theatre Company. His Modern training was further enhanced at the Noble Douglas Dance Company where he studied the Graham technique. His prowess is the result of years of Classical ballet (RAD) training at the Caribbean School of Dance.

The consummate professional, Quint continued to expand his knowledge of various disciplines with extensive training in traditional African & Caribbean Folk Dance at the Barataria Council and the La Chappelle Dance Company.A recipient of the Best Choreographer Award for the Community Development Program, Quint has been teaching for over 14 years and has worked in numerous countries including England, Barbados, Canada, Jamaica, Trinidad, Nigeria and the U.S.A.

MELONY MOK

Creative, enthusiastic and very passionate about sharing her talents as a dancer/ dance instructor. With over 20 years of extensive training in a wide variety of dance forms including Classical Ballet (RAD), Jazz, Tap and Modern. She has trained with the Edmonton School of Ballet, Shelley's Dance Company and George Randolph School of the Arts in Toronto.

Melony has performed in various dance and musical theatre productions throughout Edmonton, Vancouver and the U.S.A. She has 10 years teaching experience mainly focused on teaching children and youth. Her love for this beautiful art form is a natural and very exciting part of her life and she is thrilled to share her passions and creativity with the world.
