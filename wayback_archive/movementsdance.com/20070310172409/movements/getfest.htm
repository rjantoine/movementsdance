What’s Going on with Movements?
Open House:November 14, 2006
G.E.T. Festival: February 20-23rd, 2007
IT’S HAPPENED!

It is with great pleasure that we invite you to attend our Open House, which will be held on November 14th 2006, 6:00 pm at our Offices located at #04, 10275 Jasper Avenue.

As you may have heard we have recently completed the renovations of our facilities and would be honoured if you would join us to celebrate. We would be delighted to have you meet our new Faculty and to also share with you the excitement of our upcoming 2006/2007 Dance Season.

As always we appreciate your comments and ideas to enhance our success at Movements.

Warmest regards,
Board of Directors Movements

Movements is proud to announce the Third Annual Getting Edmonton Together Youth Festival!

On the heels of our runaway success since 2004 G.E.T. Fest 2007 will aim to educate and inspire even more Edmonton youth about dance, multiculturalism, and youth culture. Our there this year H.Y.P.E. (Helping Young People Evolve), and we’ll be visiting schools from February 20th – 23rd, 2007.


Participants can expect these highlights:

  • Celebrity keynote speakers
  • Empowering messages about leadership and high school completion.
  • Opportunities to connect with and better understand their peers.

    • Please contact G.E.T. Fest if your school wants to be part of G.E.T. Fest 2006.
